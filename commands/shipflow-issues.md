1. One verdict line with counts, e.g. `12 open — 🔴 2 critical · 🚧 1 needs-human`.
2. A table, one row per issue: `| # | Title | Priority | Labels |`. Emoji-code
   priority (🔴 critical · 🟠 high · 🟡 medium · 🔵 low).
3. No paragraphs.
